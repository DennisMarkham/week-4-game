Crystal Collector

Each crystal has been given a random number value. Click the crystal to add its value to your total. If you can match the goal, you win. If you exceed it, you will lose. Eitherway, the game will restart.

New Feature: Wins and losses will be tracked persistently! However, feel free to clear your stats at any time.
